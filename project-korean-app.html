Learn Korean with K-culture

Project goal

Create an app with which people can learn Korean culture as well as Korean language

My role

UX designer leading the project

Target audience

People who are interested in learning Korean language and culture

Key challenges

As a Korean teacher, I interviewed my students who took my Korean courses and some other people who are interested in Korea. There were a few challening points as follows:

  • There are few ways to study Korean language in relation to Korean culture.
  • The expressions one can learn from Korean language apps are not always correct and appropriate to use.

User testing results

I conducted interviews that revealed a few pain points for users.

  • The icons on the "Word List" page seem confusing.
  • There are too many options when a word is clicked.
  • It is not easy to know what "Language" on the menu means.
